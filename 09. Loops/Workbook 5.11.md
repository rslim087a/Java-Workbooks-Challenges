Task 5
------

Whenever the user rolls a **6**:
   -   print: `End of game.`
   -   stop the game.

Whenever the user rolls a 4:
  -   print: `Zero points. Keep rolling.`

When the user rolls anything else:
  -   update the `score` variable by 1

  -   print: `One point. Keep rolling`.
