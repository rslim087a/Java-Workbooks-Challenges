```java
"Name: " + <name field> + "\n" + "Nationality: " + 
<nationality field> + "\n" + "Date of Birth: " + 
<date of birth field> + "\n" + "Seat Number: " +
<seat number field> + "\n" + "Passport: " + 
<toString of passport field> + "\n";
```
